10.Om een tabel te maken heb je volgende elementen nodig: HTML tables

<table > </table > = is table is an arrangement of data in rows and columns.

<thead > </thead > = is a row at the top of a table.

<tbody > </tbody > = is main body content of the table

<tfoot > </tfoot > = footer area of the table

<th > </th > = the head section of the table

<tr > </tr > = the horizontal section of the Table Row

<td > </td > = the main information of the table inside the body section of the Table
